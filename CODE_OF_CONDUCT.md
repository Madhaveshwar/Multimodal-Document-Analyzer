# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive experience for everyone who interacts with this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Share knowledge generously.
- Keep communication professional and inclusive.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Doxxing, threats, or stalking
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or pull requests

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and any project-related community spaces.
